Lessons and Availability

Group Course

Clay by Dallin offers a variety of ways for anyone to learning pottery-regardless of experience! Our most popular option is the Beginner's group course. This course is perfect for anyone with little to no experience on the potter's wheel, and each student comes away from this two hour lesson with at least one piece of their own making. Whether you're looking for a fun date-night, or just wanting to test the waters before committing to private lessons, this is the option for you!

Join a Group Lesson!






Private Lessons

Not a fan of group lessons? Not a problem! We also offer private lessons with none other than Dallin himself! Unlike the group lessons, these private sessions are catered to the individual. If you've never touched clay before, we can get you started! If you've got a few years under your belt, but are trying to learn a few more tricks--we've got you covered. Private lesson availability is determined on a first-come-first-serve basis.

Book a Private Session!






Advanced Techniques

Our last option is our Advanced Technique workshop. This is a short class that takes place every Saturday. We cycle through a variety of techniques, with each Saturday focusing on a different skill! Common topics are sculpting, glazing, carving, sgraffito, trimming, and handle making. If you've already got some experience and are looking for ways to up your pottery game, these classes may be just what you're looking for!

Sign-up for Advanced Techniques!
