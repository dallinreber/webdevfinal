about

Hello! My name is Dallin Reber, and I am the face behind clay by dallin. It all started with a lump of clay and a love for adventure-- trying to use my life and experiences to inspire my art..

I started studying ceramic art in highschool, and while I certainly was no prodigy, I immediately fell in love. I took the class as many times as I could during the school year, and during the summer months I maintained a membership at a local studio. I wanted to learn everything I could about pottery.

After graduating, I had some important decisions to make. Was pottery really going to be able to support the kind of lifestyle I wanted? Would I be able to make enough money to pay rent? Unfortunately, I decided that pottery was probably something that I should only do as a hobby, so I stopped creating. This story, however, has a happy ending! After several years of doing pottery off and on, I realized that I wasn't ever going to be able to quit. I spoke with some mentors and dear friends of mine, and I got the courage I needed to take my artwork to the next step. I know work as an instructor and a studio here in northern Utah, and I'm loving every minute of it. I still have the ability to work on my own pieces, while sharing this part of me with others.
